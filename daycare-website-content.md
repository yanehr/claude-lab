## Enrollment Process

### How Enrollment Works

We keep this process clear and direct — because your time matters, and the right fit should be obvious when you find it.

---

**Step 1 — Reach Out**

Fill out the short inquiry form below or send an email to [email]. Let us know your child's age, your desired start date, and your schedule needs. You'll hear back within one business day.

---

**Step 2 — Intro Call (15 minutes)**

Before anyone visits anything, we have a brief phone or video call. This is a chance to ask your most important questions, confirm schedule alignment, and get a feel for whether this program makes sense for your family. No pressure, no commitment.

---

**Step 3 — In-Person Tour**

If the call feels right, we schedule a visit. You'll see the space, meet the provider, and observe the environment during an active program day when possible. Bring your child — their comfort here matters too.

---

**Step 4 — Enrollment Paperwork**

Once you're ready to move forward, we send over a straightforward enrollment packet: program agreement, emergency contacts, health and immunization records, and a getting-to-know-your-child form. Everything is digital and easy to complete on your own time.

---

**Step 5 — Transition Plan**

We don't do cold-start drop-offs. Every new child gets a custom transition week — shorter days at first, with space to build confidence and connection before diving into the full schedule. We'll keep you informed every step of the way.
